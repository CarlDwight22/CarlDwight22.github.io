Nasugbu, Batangas, Philippines

Nasugbu is the largest town in Western Batangas with a total land area of 27,851 hectares. The municipality is also the second largest in the province and it accounts for 8.69% of the total provincial land area. It is divided into 42 Barangays, 30 Rural Barangay and 12 Urban Barangay of which are connectively known as the Poblacion (Brgy. 1-12). Today, the Municipality of Nasugbu, Batangas is transforming into a new town by realizing its vision of becoming a city. The town has one proclaimed Special Eonomic Zone and has identified its role in agricultural production and touism development. It was declared as a Tourist Zone together with Maragondon and Ternate Cavite under Proclamation No. 1520 and a Marine Reserve under Proclamation No. 1801.

Best Beach Resorts in Nasugbu, Batangas

Are you thinking of having a vacation in the Philippines that is near Manila? Then we got you! Batangas is a popular destination for both local and foreign tourists because of its beautiful beaches and stunning mountains. And because of that, there are also tons of beach resorts in the city. Some of the best beach resorts in Batangas can be found in Nasugbu – one of the citie’s top locales. And for your next Batangas adventure, we provided you a list of the best beach resorts in Nasugbu, Batangas.

Canyon Cove Hotel & Spa

A resort that overlooks the South China Sea, Canyon Cove Hotel & Spa offers beachfront accommodation which is ideal for a holiday vacation under the beautiful sun.

Sand Bar Beach Resort

Situated in the heart of Nasugbu, Sand Bar Beach Resort is the perfect base from which to discover the city of Batangas. A variety of top-class facilities such as daily housekeeping, car park, beach, car park onsite, and self-parking can be enjoyed here.

Pamana Beach Resort

Facing the beachfront, Pamana Beach Resort features 2-star accommodations in Nasugbu and has a restaurant, shared lounge, and garden. Guests can also find barbeque, free WiFi and free private parking at the resort.

Amara Residences

Whether you’re traveling for pleasure or for business purposes, Amara Residences is the ideal resort for you. It offers many facilities to enrich your stay in Batangas. Some of the top features of the hotel include 24-hour security, car park, family room, laundry service, and dry cleaning.

Calayo Beach Resort

Ideal for fun and relaxation, Calayo beach resort is located in the Nasugbu area of Batangas. It offers a variety of facilities to enrich your stay in Batangas.

Pico Sands Hotel

Conveniently located near Manila, Pico Sands Hotel is the ideal base for guests to enjoy easy access to many of the city’s landmarks and popular attractions.

Pico de Loro Condotel

Pico de Loro Condotel in Nasugbu, Batangas offers convenience and comforts to its guests. It provides a shrine, convenience store, wheelchair accessible, 24-hour front desk.

Club Punta Fuegot

Club Punta Fuego is located only 10 kilometers away from the city center. For those of you who want to do some activities, Pico De Loro Beach and Country Club are just some of the attractions available to visitors.

Casa Amore

A resort that features air-conditioned accommodations with a private pool, garden view, and a balcony, Casa Amore is located in Nasugbu, Batangas.
